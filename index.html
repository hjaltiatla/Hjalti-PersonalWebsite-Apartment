About the Apartment

This comfortable 65 m² apartment at Skyggnisbraut 6, 113 Reykjavik is perfect for two adults, with extra space for two children on air mattresses. Conveniently located near the ring road for easy access to Iceland's attractions, downtown Reykjavík is just a 15-minute drive away. The apartment provides free parking and is close to a newly built public swimming pool, perfect for relaxing after a day of exploring. You'll also find a nearby hiking area Úlfarsfell.

The bright and airy living room focuses on comfort, offering a cozy sofa, large smart TV, and a dining table for four. It also comes with an efficient working desk and computer monitor. With a fully equipped oven, induction cook top, dishwasher, and more, the kitchen is complete. The large bedroom includes a comfortable king-size bed, with luxurious linens and ample closet storage. The bathroom is spacious, complete with a walk-in shower and a very handy washing machine/dryer. This would be a perfect apartment for a couple and two children, as an air mattress is provided to comfortably accommodate the little ones.
